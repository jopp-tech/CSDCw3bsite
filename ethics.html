AI in Dev Work: Wedding Analogy

Imagine you are organizing a wedding. The documentation team charges 40k for a package. They will use an iPhone 16 Pro Max and CapCut for same-day video edits. The team has 2 years of experience and an above-average portfolio. Would you accept the deal? Let's analyze:

  • Integrity: Even with basic tools, the work can be original and ethical if done properly.
  • Creativity: Using CapCut effectively to create compelling same-day edits demonstrates ingenuity.
  • Skill Level: 2 years of experience plus a strong portfolio justifies their pricing.
  • AI Analogy: Similarly, AI tools can produce impressive outputs quickly, but human oversight is essential to ensure quality, originality, and ethical standards.

Conclusion: You might accept the deal if the work meets your expectations. Just like the documentation team, AI can assist in producing high-quality work, but humans must review and refine outputs to maintain integrity and creativity.
