AI in Dev Work: Wedding Analogy

Imagine you are organizing a wedding. The documentation team charges 40k for a package. They will use an iPhone 16 Pro Max and CapCut for same-day edits. They have 2 years of experience and an above-average portfolio. Would you accept the deal? Here's the reflection:

  • Integrity: The work is original and reflects their skill level.
  • Creativity: Using simple tools effectively shows ingenuity.
  • Skill Level: Experience and portfolio justify their pricing.

Similarly, AI “coders” can produce high-quality outputs if used responsibly, but human review is always needed.
